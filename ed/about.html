Answering Your Common Questions

Here are answers to some frequently asked questions about our programs, including training format, requirements, and session flexibility.

How will training be imparted?

Training is conducted through recorded and live online sessions, with module-specific schedules to ensure effective learning.

What are the software & hardware requirements?

A stable internet connection, headphones, and a microphone provide the best experience for live sessions.

What if I miss a live session?

If you miss a session, the recording will be emailed to you, and you can ask questions in the next live session.

Can I access training materials anytime?

Yes, all recorded sessions and materials are accessible anytime, so you can learn at your own pace.
